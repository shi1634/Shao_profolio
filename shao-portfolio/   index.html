Artist Statement

Thomas Hi is a bassist, producer, and sound designer whose work bridges performance, composition, and technology. Inspired by film scores, jazz, classical music, and video game soundtracks, he approaches music as both narrative and emotional language.

His journey began in high school, where joining the music club and picking up the bass guitar became a defining moment. For Thomas, music is a way to connect with the world—telling stories and evoking emotions that words can’t. Whether performing, producing, or building sonic tools, he looks for the emotional thread that ties every project together.
